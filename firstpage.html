My favorite movies

Welcome to my movie page! Here are some of my all-time favorite films — movies that have stuck with me long after the credits rolled.

Whiplash (2014)

A driven young drummer enrolls at a cutthroat music conservatory where his demanding instructor will stop at nothing to push him to greatness — or destroy him trying.

  • Director: Damien Chazelle
  • Why I love it: Tense, relentless, and unforgettable.

Gravity (2013)

Two astronauts work together to survive after an accident leaves them stranded in space with dwindling resources and no way home.

  • Director: Alfonso Cuarón
  • Why I love it: Visually stunning and genuinely tense.

Ford v Ferrari (2019)

American car designer Carroll Shelby and driver Ken Miles battle corporate interference to build a revolutionary race car for Ford in hopes of defeating Ferrari at the 1966 Le Mans.

  • Director: James Mangold
  • Why I love it: A thrilling, feel-good underdog story.
